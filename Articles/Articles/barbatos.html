Barbatos on the Shelf

Ignore the amount of dust on my shelf. Please.

This is the Barbatos. I used to love to build this guy, but now everytime I see it in Gundam Evolutions I scream. Like seriously, it could very well be the best Gundam in that game. However, I am glad to report that this particular model kit is AMAZING. The articulation is NUTS and the pieces never fall off. The only problem I have are that the weapons is cast in one colour of plastic. I guess you can fix that with painting. But, the anime's rendition of the weapons were silver, black, and grey. I am not complaining thought, this kit is amazing.
